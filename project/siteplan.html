Site Plan: RayN's Poet

Site Name

RayN's Poet

"RayN's Poet" reflects my poetic identity (Raynoi) and the personal voice behind my work. It's direct, brandable, and easy to associate with a poetry gallery or personal archive.

Domain availability: raynspoet.com (to be checked)

Site Purpose

RayN's Poet will serve as an online gallery showcasing my original poetry. Visitors can explore poems by theme (e.g., healing, nature, love), read full texts, and leave feedback. The site aims to reach readers who appreciate modern, introspective poetry.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find poems about healing and spiritual growth?
  • Is there a way I can send feedback or request a custom poem?

Color Schema

Primary Background: Navy Blue #0C1C2D - used for the background of the header/footer and main panels.

Accent Color: Teal #1AB6B6 - used for headings, links, buttons, category tags, and interactive elements.

Text Color: Black #0A0A0A - used for all text content to ensure readability against the background.

Secondary Background: Ivory #FDFCFB - used for the poem containers and form backgrounds to create contrast with the primary background.

Typography

  • Playfair Display (Serif): Used for headings (e.g., <h1>), poem titles, and featured text to give a gallery feel.
  • Poppins (Sans-serif): Used for body text, navigation, form labels, and descriptions for readability and modern aesthetics.
